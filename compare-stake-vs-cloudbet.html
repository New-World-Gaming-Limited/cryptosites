Quick Verdict

Stake.com is the superior sportsbook for most crypto bettors thanks to its instant payouts, wider sport coverage, and more active community. Cloudbet is worth choosing if you are a high-roller who wants to deposit large amounts of BTC — its 5 BTC welcome bonus and high betting limits cater to serious bettors. For everyday sports betting with the smoothest experience, Stake wins. For maximum bonus value and high-stakes wagering, Cloudbet competes strongly.

Bonuses & Promotions

Stake offers a 200% matched deposit up to $1,000 with competitive wagering requirements. Its ongoing promotions include daily races, weekly challenges, and sport-specific boosted odds. Cloudbet's 5 BTC welcome bonus is one of the largest in crypto sports betting, released incrementally as you place bets — a structure that rewards consistent activity rather than one-time deposits. Cloudbet also offers zero-margin odds on select markets, effectively giving bettors better value on featured events.

Sports Coverage

Stake covers 35+ sports including mainstream options (football, basketball, tennis, MMA) and niche markets (table tennis, esports, politics). Its esports section is particularly deep, with coverage of CS2, Dota 2, League of Legends, and Valorant. Cloudbet offers 30+ sports with strong football and tennis coverage. Both provide live in-play betting with real-time odds updates. Stake's broader selection and dedicated esports hub give it the edge for variety seekers.

Crypto Support

Stake supports 18+ cryptocurrencies including BTC, ETH, LTC, DOGE, and multiple stablecoins. Cloudbet supports 12+ cryptocurrencies with a focus on major coins. Both process deposits instantly with no platform fees. Stake's wider crypto selection means more flexibility for altcoin holders, while Cloudbet's curated list covers the essentials without overwhelming users.

Security & Trust

Stake operates under a Curacao eGaming licence and has built an industry-leading reputation through mainstream sponsorships (UFC, Everton FC, Drake) and transparent operations since 2017. Cloudbet has operated since 2013, making it one of the oldest crypto sportsbooks. It holds a Curacao licence and has processed millions in payouts without reported issues. Both are trustworthy, but Stake's higher profile and sponsorship deals make it more visible and accountable.

User Experience

Stake's sportsbook interface is fast, clean, and designed for quick bet placement. The bet slip is accessible from any page, and odds format can be switched between decimal, fractional, and American instantly. Cloudbet's interface is functional and well-organised but feels slightly more traditional. Both offer responsive mobile experiences. Stake's community features — including a chat room, leaderboards, and social betting — add a layer of engagement that Cloudbet lacks.

Final Verdict: Stake.com Wins (9.5 vs 8.8)

Based on our comprehensive testing, Stake.com takes the overall edge in this comparison. However, both platforms are excellent choices — your ideal pick depends on your specific priorities.
