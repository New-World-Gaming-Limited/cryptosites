Why Use Bitcoin for Gambling?

Bitcoin is the original cryptocurrency and still the most widely accepted coin at crypto casinos. Nearly every platform in our database accepts BTC. On-chain transactions take around 10 minutes to confirm and fees can spike during congestion, but the Lightning Network now enables near-instant deposits at platforms that support it.

BTC Advantages

  • Accepted at virtually every crypto casino
  • Lightning Network enables instant deposits at supported platforms
  • No counterparty risk, true self-custody
  • Highest liquidity, easy to buy on any exchange

Considerations

  • On-chain fees spike during network congestion
  • 10-minute average confirmation time for on-chain deposits
  • Price volatility affects bankroll value

Frequently Asked Questions

Does every crypto casino accept Bitcoin?

Nearly every crypto casino accepts Bitcoin. It is the most widely supported cryptocurrency in gambling. The only exceptions are a handful of platforms focused exclusively on newer altcoins or Ethereum tokens.

Is it safe to deposit Bitcoin at a casino?

Depositing Bitcoin to a licensed crypto casino is safe as long as you verify the deposit address carefully. Always copy the address from the casino directly and double-check the first and last characters before sending.

How long do Bitcoin deposits take at crypto casinos?

On-chain Bitcoin deposits typically confirm in 10 minutes (one block confirmation). Some casinos require three confirmations, which takes around 30 minutes. Platforms that support Lightning Network deposits are near-instant.

Can I use the Lightning Network for casino deposits?

A growing number of crypto casinos support Lightning Network deposits, including Stake.com and BC.Game. Lightning deposits are near-instant and cost a fraction of a cent. You need a Lightning-compatible wallet to use this feature.

Does Bitcoin's price volatility affect my casino balance?

Yes. If you deposit $500 in Bitcoin and BTC drops 10%, your balance is now worth $450 in fiat terms. Using a stablecoin like USDT avoids this issue if price stability matters more to you than holding BTC exposure.

What is the minimum Bitcoin deposit at crypto casinos?

Minimum Bitcoin deposits vary by platform. BC.Game accepts as little as $5 equivalent. Stake.com requires $100 equivalent for the welcome bonus, but has no minimum for regular deposits. Check each platform's cashier for the current minimum.
