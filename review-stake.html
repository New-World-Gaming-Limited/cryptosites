Provably Fair & RTP

Stake Originals — the platform's own games — are all provably fair. The system uses SHA-256 hashing: before each bet, Stake generates a server seed (hashed and shown to the player), and the player sets a client seed. After the round, both seeds are revealed and the player can independently verify the outcome against the hash. Dice, Mines, Plinko, Crash, Limbo, Hilo, Keno, and Wheel all use this method.

The RTPs on Stake Originals are published openly in each game's info panel. Dice runs at 99% RTP (1% house edge), which is one of the lowest house edges available anywhere. Plinko varies by risk setting (low risk ~99%, high risk edges up). Crash and Limbo carry a 1% house edge. For the third-party slot catalogue, RTP typically falls between 95% and 97%, consistent with the industry average — Stake does not publish aggregate slot RTP reports but individual game RTPs are accessible within each game's paytable. For live table games, European Roulette runs at 97.3% RTP and standard Blackjack sits at around 99.5% with basic strategy.
