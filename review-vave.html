VIP & Loyalty Program

Vave takes a cashback-centric approach to loyalty. The core mechanism is a weekly cashback programme: players receive a percentage of their net losses back each week, paid as real money (not bonus funds), with no wagering requirements attached. The base cashback rate starts at 10% for all players and scales up to 25% at higher tiers. Weekly payouts land every Monday, making the schedule predictable.

Tiers at Vave are Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond. Progression is based on cumulative wager volume. Each tier above Bronze adds to the cashback percentage and unlocks additional perks: Silver brings priority withdrawals, Gold adds a dedicated support manager, Platinum includes monthly reload bonuses, and Diamond players receive bespoke packages with personalised bonus structures and access to exclusive tournaments. The no-wagering-requirement cashback policy is the headline feature — at most casinos, cashback comes with a 1x–5x wagering requirement that significantly dilutes the value.
