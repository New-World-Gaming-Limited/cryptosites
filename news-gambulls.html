Gambulls Integrates Jackpot Studios: 100,000 Bets Processed in First 24 Hours

Crypto casino Gambulls has completed its integration with Jackpot Studios, processing over 100,000 bets within the first 24 hours — every single one in under 250 milliseconds. The partnership signals a major infrastructure investment for the Curaçao-licensed platform.

100,000 Bets in 24 Hours, Every One Under 250ms

Jackpot Studios, the game development arm of Jackpot.bet, has gone live on Gambulls.com. According to Tarun M., CEO of Jackpot.bet and Jackpot.Studio, the integration immediately demonstrated the infrastructure’s capability: “Over 100,000 bets processed in the first 24 hours of our integration. Every single bet processed in under 250ms.”

The sub-250ms processing time is significant for crypto casino players who expect smooth, lag-free gameplay. For context, the average human blink takes 300–400ms — meaning Gambulls is now resolving bets faster than a blink of an eye.

Jackpot Studios: Built for Scale

Jackpot Studios builds casino games with a focus on backend performance and operator flexibility. Their infrastructure is designed to handle high-volume betting without degradation, making them a natural fit for crypto-native platforms where players expect instant results.

“This is what our infrastructure was built for. Scale, speed and zero friction,” said Tarun M. “Players don’t want to stare at a loading wheel, they want to get straight into the action — and operators should be facilitating exactly that.”

What This Means for Gambulls Players

The Jackpot Studios integration expands Gambulls’ game library alongside their existing 69+ providers, which already include Pragmatic Play, Hacksaw Gaming, and Evolution. Combined with their in-house Gambulls Originals (Keno, Plinko, Baccarat, Mines, Limbo, Wheel, and Dice), players now have access to an even broader selection of titles.

For a platform that launched in late 2023, processing 100,000 bets in a single day through a new integration suggests healthy player activity and growing platform adoption. Gambulls currently holds a 4.6/5 rating on Trustpilot from nearly 180 reviews.

Gambulls’ Growing Ecosystem

The Jackpot Studios partnership is part of Gambulls’ broader strategy to build out its gaming infrastructure.
