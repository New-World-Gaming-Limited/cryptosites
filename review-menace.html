Bonus & Promotions

Menace.com's 200% welcome bonus up to $500 is generous, though it comes with an 8x wagering requirement on the bonus amount. This is reasonable for a 200% match — you'll need to bet through $4,000 on a $500 bonus before withdrawing. Ongoing promotions include parlay boosts, daily specials on featured events, and a loyalty program that rewards consistent betting with VIP perks.

The reload bonus applies to sports and casino deposits, with a 3x wagering requirement at minimum odds of 1.60. This is relatively player-friendly — most platforms require 5-8x rollover. The catch is the 7-day expiry window, which means you need to bet actively to clear the bonus.

Sportsbook Coverage

The sportsbook covers football, basketball, American football, baseball, hockey, tennis, MMA, boxing, and esports. The esports section is particularly strong, covering CS2, Valorant, League of Legends, and Dota 2 with pre-match and live markets. Traditional sports coverage is adequate but lacks the depth of prop bets available at more established sportsbooks.

Football markets are the strongest vertical, with Champions League and Premier League matches carrying enough depth for serious betting. La Liga, Serie A, and Bundesliga get standard coverage. Outside the top European leagues, coverage drops off — Brazilian Serie A has limited markets, and smaller European leagues are absent.

UFC coverage is decent for a new platform, with numbered events getting full fight cards. The platform adds new sports regularly — basketball coverage expanded significantly in early 2026 with NBA player props and quarter betting.

Crypto Payments

Menace.com supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Tether, and Solana — the inclusion of SOL distinguishes it from many competitors. Deposits confirm quickly, and withdrawals process within 1 hour on average. The minimum deposit is $10 equivalent.

BTC, ETH, and USDT are the core deposit methods, with Litecoin, DOGE, and Solana also accepted. Deposits are credited after one confirmation. Withdrawals processed in under 15 minutes during our testing — competitive but not as fast as Roobet's instant payouts or Sportsbet.io's sub-5-minute times.

Security & Fairness

Operating under a Curacao license, Menace.com employs SSL encryption and basic security measures. As a newer platform (2022), it has a shorter track record to evaluate. Two-factor authentication is available but not required — we recommend enabling it.

Mobile Experience

The mobile website is functional and responsive. Navigation is straightforward, and bet placement works well on mobile. The design leans into the brand's edgy aesthetic with dark themes and bold typography that translates well to smaller screens.

Customer Support

Live chat support is available during business hours, with email support for off-hours queries. Response times are acceptable but not industry-leading. A more detailed FAQ section would help.

Sports Markets & Coverage

Menace.com launched in 2024 and its sportsbook is still in the early growth phase. The platform covers 20+ sports with football, basketball, tennis, and UFC as the core. Football coverage includes the top European leagues (Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1) and Champions League with 80-120 markets per top match.

NBA and NFL get game-level markets with basic player props. Tennis covers ATP/WTA main draws. UFC features match winner, method of victory, and round betting for numbered events. Niche sports are limited — you won't find darts, snooker, or handball here yet.

Menace's crypto-native approach means deposits and withdrawals are fast (BTC, ETH, USDT), and the platform clearly targets the web3-native bettor who values speed over market depth. As the platform matures, expect sports coverage to expand.

Live Betting & Streaming

Menace runs 30-80 live events on a typical day. The live interface is basic but functional with odds updating every 3-4 seconds. Football and basketball get live markets with 15-25 options per match. Bet acceptance takes 2-3 seconds.

Live streaming is not available. Animated score trackers provide basic match information. For live betting depth, Menace trails established competitors significantly — Sportsbet.io or Stake offer 5-10x more simultaneous live events.

Odds Quality & Margins

Menace's margins average 6-8% on major football markets and 8-10% on niche sports. These are among the wider margins in the crypto space, reflecting the platform's early stage and lower betting volumes.

No accumulator bonuses or odds boost promotions are currently offered. The 50% weekly reload bonus helps with bankroll, but the odds themselves are not competitive with Cloudbet, Stake, or 1xBet.

Bet Types & Cash Out

Menace supports singles and accumulators (up to 15 legs). System bets and same-game multis are not yet available. Player props are limited to basic markets on football and NBA. The bet slip is simple and fast, reflecting the platform's minimalist design.

Cash-out is available on pre-match bets but not live bets. Partial cash-out is not supported. As the sportsbook matures, we expect bet type variety to expand.

Esports Betting

Menace offers basic CS2 and Dota 2 coverage with match winner markets on Tier 1 tournaments. Market depth is minimal at 5-10 markets per match. Esports margins run 8-10%.

For esports betting, Menace is not a viable option at its current stage. Thunderpick, Stake, or even Gamdom offer significantly more depth. Menace's esports may improve as the platform grows.
