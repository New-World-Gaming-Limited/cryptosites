Step-by-Step Deposit Guide

1

Get an Ethereum Wallet

Install MetaMask as a browser extension or download the MetaMask mobile app. Create a new wallet and write down your 12-word seed phrase on paper. Never save it digitally.

2

Buy Ethereum

Sign up on Coinbase or Binance. Verify your identity. Buy ETH with a card or bank transfer. Transfer your ETH from the exchange to your MetaMask wallet address.

3

Go to Casino Cashier

Log in to your crypto casino. Navigate to Deposit and select Ethereum (ETH). The casino shows your unique ETH deposit address.

4

Send ETH to the Casino

In MetaMask, click Send. Paste the casino ETH address. Enter the deposit amount. Set gas fees to Standard or Low (no need for Fast). Confirm the transaction. ETH deposits confirm in 1-5 minutes.

5

Play

Your casino balance updates after the ETH transaction confirms. Claim your welcome bonus with code NEWBONUS if available.

Important Safety Notes

  • Always double-check the deposit address before sending. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed.
  • Never share your wallet seed phrase with anyone, including casino support staff.
  • Start with a small test deposit to verify everything works before sending a larger amount.
  • Match the network: if the casino gives you a TRC-20 USDT address, send only TRC-20 USDT, not ERC-20.
