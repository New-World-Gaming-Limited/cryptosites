How to Deposit USDT (Tether) at a Crypto Casino

A step-by-step guide for first-time crypto casino depositors.

Where to Buy USDT (Tether)

Step-by-Step Deposit Guide

1

Get a USDT-Compatible Wallet

Install Trust Wallet on your phone. It supports USDT on both Tron (TRC-20) and Ethereum (ERC-20) networks. For Ethereum-based USDT, MetaMask also works well.

2

Buy USDT

On Binance or Bybit, buy USDT with your local currency. When withdrawing to your wallet, select TRC-20 (Tron) for the cheapest fees (under $1). Withdraw to your Trust Wallet TRC-20 address.

3

Check Casino Network Support

Before depositing, check which USDT networks your casino accepts. Most accept TRC-20, ERC-20, or both. Sending on the wrong network results in lost funds.

4

Copy Casino Deposit Address

In the casino cashier, select USDT. Choose the network (select TRC-20 if available). Copy the deposit address.

5

Send USDT

In Trust Wallet, go to USDT (TRC-20). Tap Send. Paste the casino address. Enter the amount. Confirm. USDT on Tron confirms in under 3 minutes and costs under $1.

Important Safety Notes

  • Always double-check the deposit address before sending. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed.
  • Never share your wallet seed phrase with anyone, including casino support staff.
  • Start with a small test deposit to verify everything works before sending a larger amount.
  • Match the network: if the casino gives you a TRC-20 USDT address, send only TRC-20 USDT, not ERC-20.
