Bonus & Promotions

Natural8's 200% deposit bonus up to $1,000 is double what GGPoker offers, making it a better entry point for crypto poker players. You also receive $120 in free tournament tickets spread over 30 days. The bonus releases through the Fish Buffet loyalty program, identical to GGPoker's system. Additional promotions include short-deck leaderboards, PLO missions, and regular $100K+ guaranteed tournaments.

Games & Traffic

As a GGNetwork skin, Natural8 shares the same player pool — meaning you get access to the same Rush & Cash tables, Spin & Gold jackpot games, All-in or Fold, and Battle Royale formats available on GGPoker. Cash games run from micro stakes to high stakes 24/7. The tournament schedule includes daily and weekly series with guarantees ranging from a few hundred to six figures.

Crypto Payments

Natural8 processes crypto deposits faster than GGPoker in our testing — typically under 15 minutes for BTC and near-instant for USDT. Withdrawals to crypto wallets average 6-12 hours. The minimum deposit is just $10 equivalent, lower than most competitors.

Security & Fairness

Natural8 uses the same security infrastructure as GGPoker, including AI-powered anti-collusion systems, real-time fraud detection, and RNG certification. While operating under a Curacao license (less strict than IoM/UKGC), the shared GGNetwork infrastructure means the actual security measures are identical.

Mobile Experience

The Natural8 app mirrors GGPoker's mobile client in functionality and design. Available on iOS and Android, it supports multi-tabling, portrait and landscape modes, and includes all cash game and tournament features. Performance is smooth even during multi-table sessions.

Customer Support

Support is available via live chat and email. Response times average 5-10 minutes for live chat. The support team is knowledgeable about crypto-specific queries and can assist with blockchain payment issues directly.
