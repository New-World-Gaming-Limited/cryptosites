Wallet Type and Design

Ledger is a Hardware (Cold Storage) wallet with a price range of $79 - $279. It supports 5,500+ across Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, Cardano blockchains. The wallet features a secure element chip (CC EAL6+ Certified Secure Element) for key protection.

Security Features

Ledger uses proprietary firmware. Mobile app available for on-the-go management. The wallet supports DeFi protocols and NFT storage.

Payments and Crypto

Ledger accepts multiple cryptocurrencies including BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, LTC, BNB, MATIC, DOGE. Deposits are fast and withdrawals take a reasonable timeframe.
