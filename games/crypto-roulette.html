Types of Roulette Available at Crypto Casinos

Crypto casinos offer the same roulette variants as traditional casinos: European (single zero, 2.7% house edge), American (double zero, 5.26% house edge), and French (single zero with La Partage rule, reducing the effective edge on even-money bets to 1.35%). Always choose European or French over American roulette.

Live dealer roulette from Evolution Gaming is available at most top crypto casinos including Stake, BC.Game, and Cloudbet. Evolution's Lightning Roulette adds random number multipliers that can boost wins up to 500x on individual numbers. Speed Roulette runs 80 rounds per hour instead of the typical 40.

Crypto Roulette Advantages

Betting with crypto on roulette gives you the same house edge as fiat roulette but with faster cashouts and lower minimum bets at some platforms. BC.Game allows bets as small as $0.10 equivalent. Withdrawal of winnings takes under 10 minutes at most top-rated platforms, compared to 1-3 days for bank transfers.

Roulette Strategy

No strategy changes the house edge, but bankroll management helps. The Martingale (doubling after a loss on even-money bets) works short-term but requires an unlimited bankroll to work indefinitely. The D'Alembert (increasing by one unit after a loss) is gentler. Flat betting is the most rational approach for entertainment-focused play.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of roulette has the lowest house edge?

European roulette has a 2.7% house edge. French roulette with the La Partage rule reduces this to 1.35% on even-money bets. American roulette has a 5.26% house edge and should be avoided if European is available.

Can I play live roulette with Bitcoin?

Yes. Most top crypto casinos offer live roulette powered by Evolution Gaming or Pragmatic Play Live. You deposit Bitcoin or another crypto, and bet at live tables with real dealers. Cashouts are in crypto and process quickly.

Are there provably fair roulette games?

Traditional roulette (wheel and ball) is not provably fair because the randomness comes from physical mechanics in live games or certified RNG in software games. Some crypto casinos offer blockchain-based roulette variants that are provably fair, but these are less common than provably fair dice or crash games.

What is the minimum bet for crypto roulette?

Minimum bets vary by platform and table. Software roulette often allows bets from $0.10-$1 equivalent. Live roulette tables typically start at $0.50-$5. VIP tables have higher minimums and higher maximum bets.

How do I withdraw roulette winnings in crypto?

After winning, go to the cashier or withdrawal section of the casino, enter your crypto wallet address, select the amount, and confirm. Most top crypto casinos process withdrawals in under 10 minutes.
