How Crypto Dice Works

Crypto dice is elegantly simple. You pick a number between 1 and 99, choose whether the roll will be over or under that number, and bet. If the outcome matches, you win at odds determined by the probability. Choosing under 50 gives roughly 2x payouts. Choosing under 10 gives around 10x. The math is transparent.

The house edge on most crypto dice games is 1%. Stake Dice, BC.Game Dice, and Roobet Dice all run at 1%. This is far lower than most casino games (slots run 3-10% house edge, roulette 2.7-5.26%).

Provably Fair Verification

Dice was one of the first provably fair games and the easiest to verify. Before each roll, the casino shows you a hashed server seed. You provide a client seed. After the roll, the server seed is revealed and you can recalculate the dice outcome using HMAC-SHA256. Stake, BC.Game, and Roobet all offer one-click verification.

Dice Betting Systems

Because dice has a pure mathematical structure, it attracts systematic bettors. The Martingale (doubling after a loss) is common but dangerous on losing streaks. The Hi-Lo system (alternating high and low targets) reduces variance. Flat betting at 1% house edge gives the best long-term expected value per unit of risk.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the house edge on crypto dice?

The standard house edge on provably fair crypto dice is 1%. This applies to Stake Dice, BC.Game Dice, Roobet Dice, and most other provably fair dice games. It is one of the lowest house edges available in gambling.

How do I verify a crypto dice roll?

After a roll, click the 'Verify' button on the game. The casino reveals the server seed, and you can check that it matches the hash shown before the bet. Run HMAC-SHA256 on the server seed plus client seed plus nonce to confirm the dice result was fair.

Can I make a living playing crypto dice?

No. All dice games have a house edge, which means the expected value per bet is negative. Skilled bankroll management can extend your playing time, but the house edge ensures that over enough bets, the casino always comes out ahead.

What is the minimum bet for crypto dice?

Minimum bets are very small. Stake and BC.Game allow bets starting at $0.0001 equivalent. This makes dice one of the most accessible games for players with small bankrolls.

Which platforms have the best crypto dice?

Stake.com's Dice is the most polished, with excellent auto-bet features and real-time statistics. BC.Game Dice is popular for its higher bet limits. Roobet Dice is simpler but has a loyal player base.
