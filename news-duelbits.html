Duelbits Launches Enhanced Sportsbook With Esports Focus and Daily Rewards

The crypto casino and sportsbook overhauls its sports section with dedicated esports markets, improved live betting, and a daily reward system offering free bets and cashback.

Brands in this story: Duelbits

Revamped Sportsbook

Duelbits has relaunched its sportsbook with a cleaner interface, faster odds updates, and expanded market coverage. The platform now covers over 25 sports with dedicated sections for CS2, Dota 2, Valorant, and League of Legends esports.

Live betting has been significantly improved with real-time match statistics, one-click bet placement, and cash-out functionality on both pre-match and in-play bets.

Daily Reward System

The new daily reward system gives every active player a free reward to claim each day, ranging from free bets and bonus funds to cashback percentages and free spins. Rewards scale with the player's activity level, with more valuable rewards unlocked by consistent play.

The system replaces the previous weekly bonus structure and is designed to encourage daily engagement without requiring large wagers.

Casino Updates: 4,000+ Games

The casino section has grown to over 4,000 games with the addition of providers including Hacksaw Gaming, Nolimit City, and Push Gaming. Duelbits' original games (Crash, Dice, and Coinflip) continue to be popular among the platform's core audience.

The welcome bonus of 100% up to $1,000 applies to first deposits via any supported cryptocurrency, with 35x wagering requirements.
