March 2026 Withdrawal Speed Test: We Tested 15 Crypto Casinos

Our monthly withdrawal speed test results are in. See which platforms deliver the fastest crypto payouts.

SN
Sarah Nakamura
Testing Lead · 2026-03-12

Every month, our testing team deposits real cryptocurrency into the platforms we review and then withdraws it, measuring exactly how long the process takes from request to confirmed receipt. This month, we tested 15 of the most popular crypto casinos and sportsbooks. Here are the full results for March 2026.

Methodology

To ensure consistency and fairness, we follow a strict testing protocol:

  • All withdrawals are made from fully verified accounts with completed wagering requirements
  • We withdraw in Bitcoin (BTC) to a standard Ledger hardware wallet address
  • Timing starts when the withdrawal request is submitted and ends when the transaction has 3 blockchain confirmations
  • We test at off-peak hours (Tuesday, 10am UTC) to minimise network congestion variables
  • Each platform is tested 3 times; we report the median result
  • Withdrawal amounts are standardised at approximately $500 USD equivalent in BTC

Key Takeaways

Instant processing is the new standard. Stake, BC.Game, and DuelBits all processed withdrawal requests instantly — meaning the BTC transaction was broadcast to the network within seconds of the request. For these platforms, the only delay is blockchain confirmation time, which is outside their control.

Mega Dice is the surprise improver. Last month, Mega Dice had a 25-minute processing time. This month, they have cut it to 5 minutes, placing them solidly in the “Excellent” tier. This kind of improvement suggests active investment in their payment infrastructure.

Americas Cardroom remains the outlier. With a 45-minute processing delay before the BTC is even broadcast, ACR continues to trail the field. Their customer support explained this as a manual security review process, which may provide protection but significantly impacts user experience.

Lightning Network Adoption

An increasing number of platforms now support Lightning Network withdrawals, which can bring total confirmation time down to under 30 seconds. Stake and BC.Game both offer Lightning withdrawals with no additional fees. If speed is your priority and you are comfortable with Lightning wallets, this is by far the fastest option.

We plan to include a dedicated Lightning Network speed test in next month's report as adoption continues to grow.

How We Use This Data

Withdrawal speed is one of the 10 categories in our rating methodology. Platforms that consistently deliver fast, reliable withdrawals score higher in our reviews. If a platform's withdrawal times deteriorate, we update our ratings to reflect the change — because your ability to access your funds is a fundamental measure of platform quality.
