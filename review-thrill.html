Bonus & Promotions

Thrill.com offers first-bet insurance up to $500 — if your first sports bet loses, you get the stake refunded as a bonus with a 1x wagering requirement (essentially a free second chance). Ongoing promotions include accumulator boosts, early payout on leading teams, and weekly free bet offers for active users.

Sportsbook Coverage

The sportsbook covers 20+ sports including football, basketball, tennis, American football, baseball, hockey, cricket, rugby, boxing, MMA, and esports. Live betting is smooth with real-time odds updates. The odds themselves are competitive — our comparison testing showed Thrill.com consistently within 1-2% of the best available prices on major markets.

Crypto Payments

Thrill.com accepts Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Tether. Deposits confirm quickly (within 10 minutes for BTC, near-instant for USDT), and withdrawals average under 30 minutes. The minimum deposit is $15 equivalent. The crypto-focused checkout is clean and straightforward.

Security & Fairness

Operating under a Curacao license, Thrill.com employs SSL encryption, 2FA, and cold storage for the majority of crypto assets. The platform has passed third-party security audits and publishes its responsible gambling policies prominently.

Mobile Experience

The mobile web experience is Thrill.com's highlight. The responsive design is fast, bet slips work smoothly, and live betting updates in real-time without lag. No native app, but the mobile site is better than many dedicated apps.

Customer Support

Live chat and email support are available 18 hours per day. Response times are adequate, though 24/7 coverage would be preferred for a platform targeting global users across time zones.
